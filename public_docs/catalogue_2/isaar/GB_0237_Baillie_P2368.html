Baillie | John | 1829-1891 | Free Church minister

Biographical Information

Occupation, Sphere of Activity

John Baillie was born at Beauly, Inverness-shire in 1829, the son of Peter Baillie and Christina Fraser. After studying at both the University of Edinburgh and Free Church College, Edinburgh, he was ordained at Moy, Inverness-shire, in 1862. In 1875 he moved to Gairloch and 10 years later married widow Annie Mackenzie, (nee Macpherson). He died at Liverpool in 1891.

Relationships

John Baillie had 3 sons. John Baillie (1886-1960) was the internationally reknowned theologian. Donald Macpherson held the Chair of Systematic Theology at the University of St. Andrews. Peter Baillie (1889-1914) was a missionary doctor.

His nephew was Sir Duncan Colvin Baillie (1856-1919), colonial administrator.
